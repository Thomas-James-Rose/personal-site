Welcome To Platform 22

We are a three piece acoustic trio based in Northamptonshire. We play many covers from the likes of Passenger, Ed Sheeran, Pigeon Detectives and lots more; as well as a handful of original tracks!

Our new EP titled "Mustard Socks" is currently in the works! Coming 2017.
